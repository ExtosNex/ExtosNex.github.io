Duke Of Edinburgh Camp

We left from school with haste and began the long journey camp in which we would learn the importance of leadership and adventure in life. We stopped at a Mountain Biking trail to tackle our first challenge. We rode mountain bikes inside the wet forest. It took more than our strength to accomplish this feat. Everyone morally supported each other to get through this course.

After that, we took another long bus ride to Pisces Caravan Park where we would set up our tents for the first night. Unfortunately, I had caught a mild flu and had to be sent to the Otway Health Hospital. They hooked up an IV bag into me and sent me home right after that. That itself was a pretty daunting experience for me.
