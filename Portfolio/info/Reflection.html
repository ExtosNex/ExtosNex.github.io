Stress Management

In this workshop we went through several ways to reduce levels of stress in everyday life such as exercise. Exercise is a key approach in keeping us relaxed as it takes our mind of things as they release Endorphins which is one of our brain’s happy chemical signals.

I found this workshop will become really useful to students alike as we’re not too far away from exams and VCE Subjects. This gave me an insight to what the best ways to deal with stress is really helpful. With the workload that is associated with higher studies this workshop was very relevant and helpful.

Driver’s Education

We learned more about driving and hazards. We identified various types of hazards that we may be facing. We learned the Four stages of learning how to drive with a Learning Permit. We watched dash cams to see examples of hazards and things to look out for.

This was truly an eye opening experience as cars are just portrayed as just a simple form of transportation. But in reality, they hold the lives of those who are in and around the vehicle.
